# 💧 Overwatering Plants: Signs, Causes, and Smart Solutions

## Introduction

Overwatering is one of the most common mistakes in plant care. Many plant owners assume more water means better growth, but in reality, excess water can damage roots and lead to plant decline.

Understanding the signs of overwatering is essential for maintaining healthy plants.

---

## ⚠️ Signs of Overwatering

- Yellowing leaves  
- Soft or mushy stems  
- Root rot  
- Moldy soil surface  
- Drooping despite wet soil  

These symptoms are often confused with other issues.

---

## 🧠 Why Overwatering Happens

- Watering on a fixed schedule instead of checking soil  
- Poor drainage systems  
- Using pots without holes  
- Misunderstanding plant needs

## 🌱 How to Fix Overwatering

- Let soil dry before watering again  
- Improve drainage  
- Use appropriate soil mix  
- Adjust watering frequency  

---

## 🔍 Prevention Tips

- Always check soil moisture before watering  
- Use pots with drainage holes  
- Avoid fixed watering schedules
